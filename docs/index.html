About Us and Diskreet

Diskreet Messenger was born when one of our founding developers started a passion project focusing on the benefits of anonymity, chat security, and privacy at the forefront of everything the messenger does. But then we realised that nobody's going to use that, so another one of us started developing a front-end interface.

Diskreet is based in the Land Down Under, or the Great Nation and Continent of Australia. We strive to achieve features and security measures that no other messaging app has done before, and to be open and transparent to our users in as many ways as possible.

Efficient and Tiny: ReactJS and NodeJS

Diskreet follows behind open-source projects such as Mozilla Firefox. We do not install anything onto your device when the client is used. No junk files, no cache, no cookies (or biscuits), nothing.

Secure and Protected

Diskreet is completely built on the base philosophy of anonymity. It is engineered particularly to allow complete control for the user and guarantee security throughout the entire messaging process. We see nothing that goes into our servers, and nothing that comes out of them.

Fully Configurable Server and Client

Don't want animations? Sure. Don't want colours? Sure. Want to get rid of the Server IP configuration and use the default one? Be our guest. Don't want the chat box? Absolutely no problem. Want to uninstall the entire backend? Just do it.

Integrated Quality-Of-Life Functions

All the features you expect from a messaging client, but beefed up and on crack. URL link clicking straight from the chat, multi-room support, and permanently wiping the server-side chat with just an effortless click, Diskreet has you covered.
